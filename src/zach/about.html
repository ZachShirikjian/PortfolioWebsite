HI THERE!

I am a Game Design graduate from Fitchburg State University (FSU). Working with Unity Engine
since 2017, I acquired interests in UI/UX Design, C# Programming, and Graphic Design.
The goal behind my designs is to aesthetically match the visual style of the project, while
being easily understandable to players.

Outside of development, I am a game history enthusiast, advocating for proper preservation
efforts of video games. Package design helps me achieve this goal, as I create custom game
cases for myself and for other people's projects. I also create videos discussing the user interfaces
of games on my YouTube channel, AquaTwist.

I can be reached via zshirikjian@gmail.com or 339-832-5175.
